Grammar

Vocabulary

和 hé

你和我

nǐ hé wǒ

You and I

老板喜欢咖啡和茶。

Tā de bàba qù zhōngguóle

The boss likes coffee and tea

今天和明天都可以吗 ？

Jīntiān hé míngtiān dōu kěyǐ ma?

Is today and tomorrow both ok?

我和你一样 。

Wǒ hé nǐ yīyàng.

I'm the same with you

她和她哥哥一样高。

Tā hé tā gēge yīyàng gāo.

Her and her brother are the same height
